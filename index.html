Joseph Henderson

joseph@josephhenderson.me

josephhenderson.me

I have been deeply aware that there was something wrong with how we treated the planet for my entire living memory. I believe we must now do everything in our power to look after this beautiful world and galvanise others to do the same. Between Art, Policy making and Science there’s a possible solution to the disregard we have for this world; and I’m determined to spend my life being part of that.

Through my art, I journey to find hope and unity at a time when I’m most worried about what our collective future holds.
